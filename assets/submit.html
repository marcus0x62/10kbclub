Submit a Site

If you know (or run) a site that is smaller than 10KiB, please submit it below.

Please note the following

  1. This site uses the compressed size as the eligibility criteria. Use your browser tools to verify the 'transferred size' from a cold start (no cache) before submitting.
  2. Submitted sites will be manually reviewed. Sites must host some interesting content (i.e., not just be a contact page, or a link to other content that would not otherwise qualify.
  3. Interesting doesn't necessarily mean 'lots of writing' -- demos of clever CSS and JavaScript hacks are welcome. Blogs/microblogs are great, too.
  4. Sub-pages are acceptable if they represent a unique application, site, or are otherwise distinct or separate from the domain they are hosted on.
  5. Please submit sites in the form 'http[s]://site.name/[page]'
