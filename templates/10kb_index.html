The 10KB Club

The 10kb club is an index of interesting small websites, all with home pages less than 10KiB, or 10,240 bytes. By default, the sites are sorted based on user votes, rather than size, to showcase sites that are truly interesting, rather than just tiny. If there are noteworthy Hacker News or Lobsters links for a site, those will be linked alongside the site in an 'interesting links' tag. Read the FAQ for more details on how 10kb.club is different than other small site lists (including the previous incarnation of the 10KiB website list.)

Feel free to submit a new site using the link at the top!

If you like this, check out these other clubs:

  • The 512KB Club
  • The 1MB Club
